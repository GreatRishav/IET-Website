History

The Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) was formed in 2006 from the merger of two historic organizations, the IEE and IIE, to become one of the largest engineering institutions globally. With over 150,000 members, the IET advances engineering through knowledge sharing, professional development, and advocacy, supporting innovation across industries like energy and healthcare

Why Us

The novelty of the TMSL IET Student Chapter is its focus on practical, hands-on learning through workshops, industrial visits, and real-world projects. It bridges academia and industry, encouraging students to innovate and develop solutions for real-world problems while gaining exposure to cutting-edge technology and a global professional network.
