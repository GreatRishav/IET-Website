What is IET?

The Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET) is a global organization dedicated to advancing engineering and technology worldwide. The IET promotes innovation, research, and knowledge-sharing, fostering a professional network that supports engineers at all stages of their careers.

TMSL Student Chapter

By connecting students with industry professionals, the TMSL IET Student Chapter prepares its members to become future leaders in their respective fields, encouraging them to explore, innovate, and contribute to the technological landscape.

Our Achievements

SmartIrrigation

On 15th March, 2024, students from TMSL participated in the National Workshop on Telecommunication and Space Technology (TELECAST-2024), showcasing their project on IoT-based smart irrigation. Their innovative approach to water management impressed the judges, earning them recognition and valuable feedback from industry experts.

Classroom Automation

The TMSL team presented their paper on Classroom Automation at the IEEE 3rd International Conference on Control, Instrumentation, Energy & Communication (CIEC) held in Kolkata on April 20, 2024. Their innovative solutions aimed at enhancing the educational experience were well-received by attendees.

HealthMonitor

On 5th February, 2024, TMSL students showcased their HealthMonitor project at the International Conference on Medical Technologies, highlighting advancements in remote patient monitoring. Their presentation garnered significant interest, demonstrating the potential impact of technology in healthcare.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

At the Sustainability Summit held on June 12, 2024, TMSL students presented their innovative eco-friendly packaging solutions. Their project aimed at reducing plastic waste received praise from environmental experts and was recognized for its potential in promoting sustainability.

AI for Retail

On 10th April, 2024, TMSL's AI for Retail project was presented at the Tech Expo, where students showcased how artificial intelligence can enhance customer experience. Their interactive demo attracted numerous visitors and sparked discussions about the future of retail technology.

Water Quality Monitoring

During the Environmental Conference on July 8, 2024, TMSL students presented their IoT-based Water Quality Monitoring project. Their innovative approach to tracking water quality parameters in real-time was well-received, emphasizing the importance of environmental awareness.

Smart Home Automation

At the Tech Innovation Fair on 22nd May, 2024, TMSL students unveiled their Smart Home Automation project. Their system, designed for energy efficiency and convenience, received accolades for its practicality and user-friendly interface.

Robotics for Education

On 28th September, 2024, TMSL's Robotics for Education project was showcased at the National Robotics Competition. Their interactive robot designed to assist in teaching basic programming concepts captured the attention of judges and educators alike.
